The French Trans Touch™

Born from the streets of Paris and the vibrant spirit of self-expression, tsgabrielle® merges classic French tailoring with bold, transformative aesthetics.

We believe in the power of visibility. Our designs are crafted not just to fit a body, but to celebrate a journey. It is a unique intersection of heritage and identity in high fashion, creating a silhouette that speaks before you do.

Gabrielle's Vision

Gabrielle envisioned a world where luxury fashion is accessible and affirming. Her vision transcends traditional binaries, offering fluidity in every stitch and seam.

"I wanted to create something that felt like home," she says. "A wardrobe that doesn't ask you to compromise who you are, but amplifies it."
